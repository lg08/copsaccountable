About CopsAccountable

A site designed to encourage community oversight of and involvement with the police.

This site was made by the Princeton Men's Soccer class of 2023 in an effort to hold police officers around the country accountable for their actions, both good and bad. Unfortunately, sites like this are incredibly necessary given the injustices that are still occuring in 2020.
